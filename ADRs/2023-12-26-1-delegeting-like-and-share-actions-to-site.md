## Decision

Delegate LIKE and SHARE actions to site.


## Description

LIKE and SHARE buttons on browser extension's popup page are hard to reconfigure, due to publish (and review) processes. So they should open
a https://negotiate.ninja/actions/like or https://negotiate.ninja/actions/share instead.
