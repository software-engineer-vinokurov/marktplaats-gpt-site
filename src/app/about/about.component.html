About

Negotiate Ninja is a groundbreaking browser plugin designed to revolutionize the way you negotiate online. At its core, Negotiate Ninja is an AI-powered assistant, utilizing the advanced capabilities of OpenAI's GPT-4 to provide real-time, context-aware suggestions for your online sales conversations. Whether you're a seasoned seller tired of repetitive queries or a novice looking to refine your negotiation tactics, Negotiate Ninja is your go-to tool for crafting compelling responses that resonate with your buyers.

What sets Negotiate Ninja apart is its unique ability to seamlessly integrate into any online chat environment, offering tailored advice and responses that evolve with the conversation. It's like having a negotiation expert by your side, providing insights and suggestions tailored to each interaction. This not only saves time but also enhances the quality of your communications, ensuring you never miss an opportunity to make a positive impression or close a sale effectively.

Designed with simplicity and efficiency in mind, Negotiate Ninja is perfect for online marketplaces, particularly during our beta phase on marktplaats.nl. Our freemium model is designed to be accessible, providing weekly credits for users to experience the full range of our service's capabilities. This innovative approach to online negotiation not only simplifies your sales process but also empowers you with the tools to negotiate smarter, not harder.

History and Background

The story of Negotiate Ninja began with a simple yet innovative idea. Faced with the challenge of frequent and often unreasonable discount requests on marktplaats.nl, the concept for Negotiate Ninja was born on a Friday morning in October 2023. The initial development led to the creation of a command-line tool that could list marktplaats conversations and suggest responses using ChatGPT, transforming the way negotiations were handled on the platform.

The project evolved quickly. The tool was integrated into a Telegram Bot, becoming a sophisticated selling assistant capable of fetching messages, providing product descriptions from marktplaats.nl, and offering enhanced ChatGPT suggestions for responses. This feature allowed users to regenerate suggestions if needed, adding a layer of customization and control.

Early success was evident. Even in its initial version, the tool demonstrated its potential by facilitating a successful deal, showcasing the effectiveness of ChatGPT's elaborate and persuasive answers. As development progressed, new features were added, including the ability to change the context used by ChatGPT, save authentication cookies for marktplaats.nl, and manage user activation and quota on OpenAI usage. These advancements were critical steps towards making Negotiate Ninja a paid service, with a focus on user experience and sustainability.

The journey of Negotiate Ninja has been marked by innovation and user-focused development, with ongoing efforts to partner with marktplaats.nl for API integration and expand the service's capabilities. From its inception, Negotiate Ninja has been more than just a tool; it's a testament to the power of AI in enhancing online negotiation and a commitment to making digital interactions more efficient and effective.
